how I spend my free time

web developement

I have really been enjoying learning web developement, it has been really challenging and overall rewarding. It takes up like all of my time and I'm not mad about it.

climbing

My favorite way to spend my time is climbing, any kind of climbing, especially outdoors. I love the focus, strategy and determination it takes to get to the top. More than the physical challenge is the mental challenge, you have to trust yourself and have the confidence to go for something that may seem out of reach. These skills adapt easily into other parts of my life as well, and that's pretty cool.
